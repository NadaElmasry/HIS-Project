OB-GYN - Best Care, Best Service. For her.


Our Obstetrics, Gynecology & Women’s Health Institute is committed to providing world-class care for women of all ages. We offer women's health services, obstetrics and gynecology throughout Giza and beyond. We work closely with them to offer treatment recommendations and follow-up care to help you receive the best outcome.

Why choose us? High quality care, because we care!

Our services are designed to meet the unique and changing medical needs of women from adolescence to mature adulthood. Our team offers coordinated and supportive care for the medical concerns that affect women’s lives, from general gynecologic care, childbirth, infertility, incontinence, gynecologic cancers, pelvic floor disorders, menopause and more. Each year we see more than 400,000 patient visits, perform over 8,000 surgeries and are involved in more than 10,000 births.
